About WebZen

WebZen is a boutique web design and development agency based in the heart of Nepal. Founded by Mohan Ram Majhi, We specialize in creating beautiful, functional websites that help small businesses and entrepreneurs establish a strong online presence.

With years of experience in web development and a deep understanding of what makes businesses succeed online, we've helped dozens of clients transform their digital presence. From local restaurants to international consulting firms, we've built websites that not only look great but actually drive results.

What sets us apart is our commitment to simplicity and effectiveness. We don't believe in overcomplicating things – we believe in creating solutions that work beautifully and help your business grow.
